Current Committee Vacancies - Get on board!!*

There are currently a few positions available. If you are interested, 
please contact Anneka Vuletich 421-0919 in the first instance.

*_President_* (Approximately three hours per month)
This involves setting the agenda for the monthly meetings and
running them, writing the president's blurb that appears in the
newsletter each month, and general support of committee.

_*Vice President*_
VP is currently unfilled. It is a support role to President and would
include assisting with committee meetings and upcoming events.
It would probably average about two hours per month.

*_Antenatal Packs_ *(three hours per month)
Ordering and photocopying pamphlets as required. You are required to 
make up two packs per couple for each antenatal course and you need the 
space to store two large containers.

_*Antenatal Leader*_
Remember your antenatal class? The person who set up and helped “glue” 
the class together? Want to emulate her? This is a paid position if one 
person will do all the classes to provide consistency. There is 
currently a total of seven per year. Otherwise, if you’re thinking of 
adding to your family or just want an antenatal update – volunteer as a 
Leader for one course (seven Wednesday nights).

*_Newsletter Editor_ *(five hours plus every second month_)_
Collate material for this newsletter from other committee members and 
own topics. All advertising is supplied by the advertising co-ordinator. 
The newsletter is currently formatted in Microsoft Publisher.

_*Baby & You Course Convener*_

There are six Baby and You courses booked for 2008. This is a very 
critical role as Baby and You forms part of the core course that HBC 
Parents Centre promotes.
It may be possible for two people to run this course.
The tasks include:
Set up in week one and pack down in week five
Booking venue – Whangaparaoa library
Confirming speakers
Making sure that speakers are paid on time
A Saturday CPR course - one per course
Reviewing course evaluations
Making sure that accurate statistics are kept
Organising hosts to assist in the following Baby and You course
